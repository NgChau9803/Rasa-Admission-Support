## PROGRAM INTRODUCTION

In the digital age, Data Science and Artificial Intelligence are becoming core fields with a strong impact on many industries. The demand for high-quality human resources in Data Science and Artificial Intelligence is very large.

Located in the strategy of changing scientific research methods and training thinking, developing the future generation of scientists in Vietnam, the Vingroup Innovation Foundation – VinBigdata Institute has signed a Master’s training cooperation agreement in Data Science with the School of Information and Communications Technology, Hanoi University of Science and Technology to support financial resources, technology, knowledge network and experts for the training institution and graduate students to have learning and research conditions that meet international standards.

# FOREIGN LANGUAGE REQUIREMENTS

Have foreign language proficiency equivalent to Level 3 or higher according to the 6-level foreign language proficiency framework for Vietnam as follows:

1. **Have a university degree or higher in a foreign language; or graduated from a university program where the language of instruction is a foreign language;**
2. **Graduated from Hanoi University of Science and Technology within the last 02 years (24 months) and the output standard meets the foreign language requirement of Level 3 or higher according to the 6-level foreign language proficiency framework for Vietnam or equivalent.**
3. **Have one of the diplomas or foreign language certificates achieving a level equivalent to Level 3 or higher according to the 6-level foreign language proficiency framework for Vietnam specified in [Appendix II](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1tavdii12PcFAn4wwVhzwGiNkwL1V_i77/view?usp=sharing) or other equivalent certificates announced by the Ministry of Education and Training, valid until the date of application. Foreign language certificates are valid for 2 years from the date of issue.**
